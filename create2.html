Create 2

The iRobot Create is an educational platform. The robots are built on refurbished iRobot Roomba vacuums, and are an affordable STEM resource to grasp the fundamentals of robotics, computer science and engineering. It can be connected to a laptop, Arduino, or RaspberryPi.
