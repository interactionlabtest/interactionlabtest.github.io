Turtlebot 2

The TurtleBot is a ground robot kit with open-source software. Our TurtleBots are equipped with Kinect sensors, so that they can see in 3D, and have netbooks so they can do all their own computations. We use them with various sensors for mapping the environment, for measuring RF signals, and many other tasks.
