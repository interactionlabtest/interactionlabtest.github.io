Get Involved!

RASC welcomes participation. Here is how to get involved with our research:

If you are a faculty member at USC and are interested in becoming a member of RASC, would like to conduct a collaborative project, or want to learn more about the Center, please email the RASC faculty member(s) of interest.

If you are a student at USC, at any level (undergrad, MS, PhD), and are interested in getting involved with RASC, please contact the relevant RASC faculty member or follow the relevant labâ€™s application procedure.

If you are a student outside of USC and would like to get involved, consider these options:

  • For undergraduates: CS Department NSF Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) Program, USC SURE, Viterbi India, some RASC members take students from the CRA-W DREU Program.
  • For high school students: SHINE
  • For others, please email the faculty member you are interested in working with and/or follow their lab application process.

    • RASC does not hire or appoint faculty, but welcomes USC robotics faculty and internal and external affiliates. Please reach out to the RASC leadership to get involved.

    If you are interested in becoming an industrial affiliate of RASC, please contact the RASC Director.
